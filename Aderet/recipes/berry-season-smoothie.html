Berry Season Smoothie

October 27, 2020

  • Summer

  • Time

    5 mins

  • Yields

    2, 8oz drinks

Ingredients

  • 1/2 banana
  • 2 cups blueberries, frozen
  • 4 blackberries, frozen
  • 1/4 cup raspberries, frozen
  • 1 heaping Tbsp peanut butter
  • 3 cups milk (sub non-dairy milk)
  • Optional: splash of cold brew and/or chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender. Blend until smooth or desired consistency. Enjoy!
